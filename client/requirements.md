## Packages
@mediapipe/pose | Body landmark detection for AR
@mediapipe/camera_utils | Camera feed handling utility
@mediapipe/drawing_utils | Helper for visualizing landmarks (optional but good for debug)
react-webcam | Simplified webcam component for React
clsx | Utility for constructing className strings conditionally
tailwind-merge | Utility for merging Tailwind classes safely

## Notes
The app requires camera permissions.
MediaPipe models are large and loaded from a CDN at runtime.
T-shirt warping logic uses HTML5 Canvas API.
